About Us

With over 21 years of experience in nurturing and educating children, Polka-Dots Daycare & Aftercare has built a legacy of hard work, dedication, and trust. We partner closely with parents to ensure each child receives personalized care, creative learning opportunities, and a safe, loving environment to grow and thrive.

Our Programs

Our Aftercare program provides a safe and nurturing environment for young children after school hours. We offer fun and educational swimming classes, engaging art projects, and interactive activities designed to stimulate creativity and learning, while ensuring each child is cared for with attention and love.

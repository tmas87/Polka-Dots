About Us

With over 21 years of experience in caring for and nurturing children, Polka-Dots Daycare & Aftercare is built on hard work, dedication, and genuine love for early childhood development. We work closely with parents to create a supportive partnership, ensuring every child feels safe, valued, and encouraged to grow emotionally, socially, and creatively.

Our Programs

Our aftercare program provides a warm, structured environment for young children after school hours. Activities include supervised swimming classes, creative art projects, and engaging play that supports learning and development. Each activity is designed to be fun, educational, and age-appropriate, ensuring children remain happy, active, and well cared for.
